Employees must reset their password through the internal Self-Service Password Portal at https://intranet/reset.
The company VPN requires Duo Mobile approval and only supports connections from corporate-managed devices.
To request new hardware, employees must submit a ticket tagged "Hardware-Provisioning" in the internal ticketing system.
The internal Wi-Fi network "CorpNet" requires a rotating weekly passphrase posted only on the secure intranet page.
Service Desk staff must escalate all Priority 1 outages to the On-Call Engineer using the internal escalation phone tree.
Multi-factor authentication resets require identity verification using the employee’s HR security PIN.
Shared mailbox access is granted only after a manager submits an approval form through the Internal Access Portal.
The laptop imaging process uses the internal PXE boot server and should not be attempted offsite.
Employees who forget their encryption key must visit the IT office in person to regenerate BitLocker recovery data.
Internal software like "FinanceTools" is distributed exclusively through the Company Software Hub at https://intranet/software.
